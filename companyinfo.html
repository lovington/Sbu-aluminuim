Company Description

Sbu aluminum is a trusted provider of high-quality window frames, door frames, and aluminum gates for residential, commercial, and industrial applications in the East Rand. Established in 2015, we have been serving our customers with excellence for 8 years. We are based in Tembisa.

Mission

To provide our customers with high-end window frames and door frames that enhance the beauty and security of their spaces. We are committed to excellence, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Through our innovation, quality craftsmanship, and outstanding services, we aim to be the trusted partner for all architectural and construction needs.

Vision

To be leaders in the aluminum window and door frame industry and be known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability. Our vision is to inspire and empower communities through beautiful, secure, and environmentally responsible solutions.
