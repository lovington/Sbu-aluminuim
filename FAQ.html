Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of choosing aluminium window frames and doors over other materials?

Aluminium is lightweight, durable, resistant to corrosion, and offers excellent thermal insulation, making it an ideal choice for window frames and doors.

What customization options are available for aluminium window frames and doors?

We offer a range of customization options, including different finishes, colours, sizes, and styles to meet your specific requirements.

How long does it take to manufacture and install aluminium window frames and doors?

The production and installation timeline depend on the project's scope and complexity. We can provide an estimated timeline upon request.

What maintenance is required for aluminium window frames and doors?

Aluminium frames and doors are low-maintenance. Regular cleaning and occasional lubrication of moving parts are usually sufficient to keep them in good condition.
